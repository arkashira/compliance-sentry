# Customer Journey Map for Compliance Sentry

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Regulatory changes in data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA) prompt organizations to seek compliance solutions.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about specific compliance requirements; overwhelming amount of information on data protection.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, anxiety about potential penalties for non-compliance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational resources (webinars, whitepapers) that simplify compliance concepts and highlight the importance of data protection.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits/downloads of educational materials.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Organizations begin researching compliance tools and frameworks to meet regulatory requirements.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in comparing different solutions; concerns about integration with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, cautious optimism about finding a suitable solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer a comparison tool or interactive demo that highlights Compliance Sentry's unique features and benefits.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests or tool comparisons initiated.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial or demo of Compliance Sentry.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity of initial setup; unclear guidance on how to utilize features effectively.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelm, curiosity about the tool's capabilities.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide personalized onboarding sessions and easy-to-follow tutorials that guide users through the setup process.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate and user engagement metrics during the trial period.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to purchase Compliance Sentry after successful trial usage.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing costs and ROI; potential resistance from stakeholders.
- **User Emotions**: Relief at finding a solution, but apprehension about long-term commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Highlight success stories and case studies demonstrating ROI and improved compliance outcomes from existing customers.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users experience the benefits of Compliance Sentry and consider expanding usage to other departments or features.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about additional features and their applicability; potential budget constraints.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction with the product, but cautious about additional investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer tailored recommendations for additional features based on user behavior and needs, along with promotional pricing for expansion.
- **Metric**: Number of upgrades or additional feature purchases.
