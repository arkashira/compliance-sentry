# Business Model Canvas for Compliance-Sentry

## Customer Segments
- Healthcare organizations (hospitals, clinics) focusing on patient data protection.
- Educational institutions (schools, universities) needing to comply with data privacy regulations.
- Technology companies developing AI solutions that handle sensitive data.
- Government agencies overseeing data protection compliance in sensitive sectors.

## Value Propositions
- Comprehensive consent management tailored for sensitive data handling in health and education.
- Automated compliance tracking and reporting to reduce manual oversight and errors.
- Enhanced data protection features to safeguard against breaches, ensuring trust and security.
- User-friendly interface that simplifies complex compliance requirements for non-technical users.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Compliance-Sentry website and dedicated sales team.
- Partnerships with industry-specific consultants and compliance experts.
- Online marketing campaigns targeting healthcare and education sectors.
- Participation in industry conferences and webinars to demonstrate product capabilities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support team for onboarding and ongoing assistance.
- Regular updates and training sessions to keep clients informed about compliance changes.
- Community forums and user groups to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.
- Personalized account management for larger clients to ensure tailored service.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing model: $500/month (approx. 17,500 THB) for small organizations; $2,000/month (approx. 70,000 THB) for larger enterprises.
- One-time setup fee for initial compliance framework implementation: $5,000 (approx. 175,000 THB).
- Additional revenue from premium features and custom integrations.

## Key Resources
- Development team with expertise in data protection and compliance regulations.
- Robust technology infrastructure for secure data handling and storage.
- Legal advisors to ensure ongoing compliance with evolving regulations.
- Marketing and sales team to drive customer acquisition and retention.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the compliance framework tool.
- Regular audits and updates to align with changing data protection laws.
- Customer training and support to ensure effective use of the tool.
- Marketing initiatives to raise awareness and attract new customers.

## Key Partners
- Legal firms specializing in data protection and compliance.
- Technology partners providing integrations with existing systems (e.g., EMR, LMS).
- Industry associations in health and education for networking and credibility.
- Regulatory bodies for insights on compliance requirements and updates.

## Cost Structure
- Salaries for development, sales, and support teams.
- Marketing and advertising expenses to promote the product.
- Technology infrastructure costs (servers, software licenses).
- Legal and compliance consulting fees to ensure adherence to regulations.
